Helping Your Child with Autism

Kootu meaning support in malayalam is an android application meant to aid for kids suffering from autism.This is an assistant for a child with autism spectrum disorder (ASD) which will help in effective communication.

Child Guide

Some children with ASD may not be able to communicate using speech or language, and some may have very limited speaking skills.It includes repeated acknowledgment of the terms that are basically needed using a special interface designed. By this,the child will be able to improve their speaking skill.Parents are able to record their own voices instead of hardcoded voice in the app.

Sleep Therapy

Sleep disorders may be even more common in children with autism spectrum disorders.There are many reasons for this such as fail to understand social cues, irregularity in release of hormone melatonin , increased sensitivity to outside stimuli, anxiety etc.This can treated by meditate with relaxation musics and other things.

Live Location

Behaviour of an autistic child cannot be predicted sometimes.This feature will help the parents to track the live location of their children and also help the child to get back home safely.Tracking can be applied with the help of wearables.

Parental guide

Emergency Chatbot will asks parents to understand the symptoms and then sends the data to doctors in an emergency situation.It will also take care of stay consistent and on schedule.
